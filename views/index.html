Private 3BHK Villa in South Goa — Hills, Forest & Beach

NIVRRITII — meaning serenity and liberation — is a luxury 3-bedroom villa for rent in Canacona, South Goa. Tucked into the lush Goan countryside, the property combines a spacious private garden, traditional Goan architecture and thoughtful modern interiors.
